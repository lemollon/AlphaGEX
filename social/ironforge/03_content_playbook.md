# Iron Forge — Content Playbook (Project A)

How the Iron Forge avatar turns the product + voice into a steady stream of posts.
**Channels (decided): X/Twitter, Instagram, and the shared Discord** (Iron Forge content
routes to `#forge-report` / `#iron-forge-bots`). LinkedIn/YouTube are optional add-ons.
Respect the compliance rules and the cross-channel strategy in `shared/05`.

---

## Content pillars (rotate across these)

1. **Build-in-public updates (the spine).** What the bots did — daily/weekly recaps,
   equity-curve screenshots (labeled paper), clean wins, honest losses, "what I shipped
   this week." The defining pillar.
2. **GEX & strategy education.** Teach one idea per post: positive vs. negative gamma,
   walls as condor edges, why VIX>32 = stand down, theta vs. gamma on 0DTE.
3. **System-design / engineering.** Sizing with Kelly, EOD discipline, PDT enforcement,
   pessimistic paper fills, "why I made the bots standalone." Hooks the engineer
   audience.
4. **Discipline & mindset.** Process over outcome, the disciplined loss, "be more like
   your bot," gratitude/humility (light faith touch).
5. **Origin & meta.** The blow-up-to-build arc, why GEX changed everything, the
   journey toward the live transition.

**Mix guide:** ~40% pillar 1, ~25% pillar 2, ~15% pillar 3, ~10% pillar 4, ~10% pillar
5. Heavy on documenting and teaching; light on selling.

## Post formats by platform

### X / Twitter (primary, high cadence)
- **Daily bot recap** (single tweet): one bot, one trade, the reasoning, the outcome,
  the disclaimer tag. Screenshot optional.
- **Teaching thread** (5–9 tweets): one GEX/strategy concept, concrete, with a SPY
  example. End with a soft CTA in the last tweet (link in reply).
- **Hot-take / myth-bust:** "Iron Condors aren't free money. Here's the day they hurt
  and why." 
- **Loss autopsy:** a red day, told straight, ending in the lesson.
- Link hygiene: keep links out of the main post; put them in a reply or bio.

### Instagram (primary, visual)
- **Carousels:** "anatomy of an Iron Condor," equity-curve recaps (labeled paper),
  "FLAME vs SPARK vs INFERNO," a 5-slide GEX mini-lesson. Last slide = soft CTA + "link
  in bio."
- **Reels:** screen-recorded dashboard walkthroughs ("watch SPARK close a 1DTE condor"),
  60-sec GEX explainers, "what my bot does that panic-me couldn't." Hook in 2 seconds.
- **Stories:** the day's bot result, a poll ("IC or stay flat today?"), behind-the-scenes
  build clips, a Discord-invite sticker.
- Use the fire/forge dark visual look; keep a consistent founder-brand frame across both
  products' posts.

### Discord (shared — Iron Forge channels)
- Post the **weekly Forge Report** to `#forge-report`; pipe live bot activity/alerts to
  `#iron-forge-bots`. Host the conversation — answer "why did INFERNO skip today?" type
  questions. Bring people in from X/IG; deepen the relationship here.

### Optional add-ons (only with spare capacity)
- **LinkedIn:** weekly long-form build update + the occasional engineering essay
  (standalone architecture, paper-fill integrity, sizing philosophy). Spell out acronyms.
- **YouTube / Reddit:** longer dashboard walkthroughs; value-first posts in r/options /
  r/thetagang per each sub's rules.

## Repeatable series (lower the per-post creative load)

- **"Forge Report"** — weekly recap of FLAME / SPARK / INFERNO (the cast format).
- **"Disciplined Loss of the Week"** — a losing trade + the rule that contained it.
  Counter-programs the entire fake-guru genre.
- **"GEX in 60 seconds"** — one micro-concept, any platform.
- **"Bot vs. Human Me"** — what the bot did vs. what panicked-2019-me would've done.
- **"Build log"** — what shipped/changed in the system this week (engineering pillar).

## Hook bank (adapt, don't overuse verbatim)

- "I built three trading bots so I couldn't sabotage myself. Here's this week's report."
- "An Iron Condor isn't free money. Here's the exact day it bit me — and the rule that
  saved the account."
- "My bots sell the bid and buy the ask on purpose. Here's why I rigged the paper math
  *against* myself."
- "0DTE isn't gambling — *unless you skip these four guardrails.*"
- "Why does price keep hitting your stop then reversing? One word: dealers. Here's the
  map."
- "FLAME, SPARK, and INFERNO walk into a market…" (the cast, lightly)

---

## Cadence (sustainable beats heroic)

- **X:** 1–2 posts/day (1 recap + 1 teach/take), 1 thread/week.
- **Instagram:** 3–4/week (mix of carousels + Reels), Stories most market days.
- **Discord:** weekly Forge Report + ongoing hosting (bot alerts are automated).
- **Always:** weekly "Forge Report" as the anchor everything else orbits.

Better to sustain 3 honest posts a week forever than burn out on 3 a day for a month.

---

## What a great Iron Forge post does (checklist)

- [ ] Teaches something true, documents the build honestly, or builds community.
- [ ] Names the bot(s) and keeps their personality consistent.
- [ ] Shows risk/the loss case, not just upside.
- [ ] Says "paper" if results are shown.
- [ ] Sounds like a person, not a brand.
- [ ] Has at most one motif/wink and zero hype.
- [ ] CTA (if any) matches the funnel ratio and points to the hub/Discord, not "profit."
